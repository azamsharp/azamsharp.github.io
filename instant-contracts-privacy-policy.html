Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

Overview

Instant Contracts respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Instant Contracts app.

Instant Contracts is designed to work without requiring you to create an account or provide personal identifying information.

Information We Collect

Instant Contracts does not collect personal information such as your name, address, phone number, or email address.

Any contract content you create (such as text, signatures, or PDFs) is created and stored locally on your device.

iCloud Sync

If you have iCloud enabled on your device, Instant Contracts may use Apple’s iCloud service to sync your saved contracts across your Apple devices.

iCloud syncing is managed entirely by Apple. Instant Contracts does not have access to your iCloud account or credentials.

How Your Data Is Used

Data stored by Instant Contracts is used solely to provide app functionality, including:

  • Creating and editing contract templates
  • Saving contracts for later access
  • Exporting contracts as PDF files
  • Syncing contracts via iCloud (if enabled)

Data Sharing

Instant Contracts does not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.

Contracts are shared only when you explicitly choose to export or send them using system features such as email or messaging.

Third-Party Services

Instant Contracts relies on Apple system services, including iCloud and in-app purchases. These services are governed by Apple’s own privacy policies.

We encourage you to review Apple’s Privacy Policy for more information on how Apple handles data.

Security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect data stored within the app. However, no method of electronic storage is completely secure.

Children’s Privacy

Instant Contracts is not intended for use by children under the age of 13, and no information is knowingly collected from children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

Privacy Policy

How Redacted handles your information.

Last updated: January 27, 2026 App: Redacted (PDF Redaction)

Redacted respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use the Redacted app.

Information We Do Not Collect

Redacted does not collect, store, track, or share personal information for advertising, analytics, or marketing purposes.

  • No user accounts are required.
  • No analytics or tracking SDKs are used.
  • No data is sold or shared with third parties.

Document Handling

All PDF documents and redactions are handled with privacy in mind.

On-device by default: Redaction actions are performed directly on your device.

On-Device Storage

By default, documents you import or create in Redacted are stored locally on your device.

CloudKit Sync (Optional)

If you enable iCloud, Redacted uses Apple’s private CloudKit database to sync your documents across your own devices.

  • Data synced via CloudKit is private and accessible only to you.
  • Redacted does not have access to your CloudKit data.
  • Apple manages CloudKit security and encryption.

Original and Redacted Documents

Redacted allows you to access both original documents and redacted versions of those documents. These remain under your control and are stored locally or in your private iCloud storage, depending on your settings.

Sharing Documents

When you choose to share a document, Redacted uses Apple’s standard system sharing tools. Documents are shared only when you explicitly initiate a share action.

Redacted does not monitor or record how or where shared documents are sent.

Data Security

Redacted relies on on-device protections provided by iOS and Apple’s CloudKit privacy and security infrastructure (if enabled). No external servers are used to process or store your documents.

Children’s Privacy

Redacted does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

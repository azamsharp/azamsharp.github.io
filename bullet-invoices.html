Frequently Asked Questions

How do I export an invoice to PDF?
Open an invoice, tap Export as PDF, then share or save the PDF using the iOS share sheet. You can preview invoices for free; PDF export requires an export purchase plan.
Can I preview invoices for free?
Yes. You can preview invoices anytime for free. Exporting to PDF requires a purchase plan: One-Time Export, Monthly, or Annual.
What’s included in One-Time Export, Monthly, and Annual?
  • One-Time Export: Export a single invoice as a PDF.
  • Monthly Subscription: Export unlimited invoices for the month.
  • Annual Subscription: Export unlimited invoices for the year (best value for frequent use).
How do I restore purchases?
Go to Settings → Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID used for the purchase.
Why aren’t my invoices syncing across devices?
Check these items:
  • iCloud is enabled on both devices.
  • Both devices are signed into the same Apple ID.
  • iCloud Drive is turned on in iOS settings.
  • Give sync a few minutes, especially after first enable.
How do I mark an invoice as paid or unpaid?
Open the saved invoice and tap Mark as Paid or Mark as Unpaid. The Saved Invoices list also supports filtering by status.
I found a bug — what should I include in my email?
Please include:
  • Device model and iOS version
  • App version
  • What happened vs what you expected
  • Steps to reproduce
  • Screenshot/video if possible

Troubleshooting

  • Export stuck? Close and reopen the invoice, then try export again.
  • PDF looks wrong? Ensure invoice fields (especially items and totals) are filled in.
  • Restore not working? Confirm the same Apple ID is signed in.
  • Sync delay? Keep the app open for a minute on both devices.

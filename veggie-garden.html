Frequently asked questions

What platforms are supported?

My Veggie Garden is built for iPhone and iPad with iCloud sync. Mac support is on the roadmap.

How do reminders work?

Set a preferred nudge timing per plant — a week before harvest, three days before, or on the harvest day. You’re in control.

What’s included in the free version?

Track up to 5 vegetables with harvest countdown and photo notes. Upgrade to Pro for unlimited plants and advanced tools.
